Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do’s:
1.Use professional and respectful language
2.Keep your profile updated and accurate
3.Share useful and informative content
4.Maintain privacy settings
5.Think before posting anything online

❌ Don’ts:
1.Do not share personal or sensitive information
2.Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language
3.Do not spread fake or unverified information
4.Avoid posting harmful or negative content
5.Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments
